# Epic 6: Story 1 - Actionable Insights

**Goal:** Surface meaningful insights without crossing into advice or liability.

---

## 6.1: Top 3 Levers (Quantified & Bounded)

**User Story:** As a user, I want to know which factors matter most.

### Built-in Guardrails
- Levers computed algorithmically
- AI explains, not selects arbitrarily

### Acceptance Criteria
- Max 3 levers
- Each includes quantified impact
- Language: "has the biggest effect" (not "you should")

---

## 6.2: "Low-Friction Wins" Identification

**User Story:** As a user, I want to see small changes with big effects.

### Built-in Guardrails
- Only considers deltas < defined thresholds
- AI forbidden from suggesting products or investments

### Acceptance Criteria
- Explains payoff in plain terms
- Includes uncertainty caveat

---

## 6.3: Assumption Sensitivity Explanation

**User Story:** As a user, I want to know which assumptions matter most.

### Built-in Guardrails
- Sensitivity analysis computed outside AI
- AI only narrates results

### Acceptance Criteria
- Highlights top 2 sensitive variables
- Encourages periodic review, not immediate action
